About Us

Hello! We are Mark and Mary Jensen. We started Greek N Go in 2015 as a restaurant, but eventually changed our venue to a food truck. We learned to make delicious Greek food from some friends in Boise, and have loved serving delicious gyros and other dishes to Provo customers. Come by and give our food a try! We'd love to meet you.
